About Us

Mission

SaniSalud works to empower women, through education and technical training, to take lead roles in developing sanitation enterprises in Nicaraguan slums.

Goals

  • Improve the quality of life for people through sustainable development of sanitation systems and health education.
  • Provide people with the basic dignity of a toilet.
  • Empower communities to take on other developmental issues

Approach

Creative Capacities of people within our own community are often underestimated and we want to give these souls a voice and a reason for participating. We seek to create a space where members of our community are encouraged to share their work and be part of a local and global community that bridges art, advocacy and science. By fostering creative expression locally, we can strengthen our own and help empower others. Art and social networking can be powerful tools to induce change and it is our goal to provide an outlet for creative minds to make an impact on sanitation development.
